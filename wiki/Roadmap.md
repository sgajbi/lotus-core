# Roadmap

## Current phase

`lotus-core` is in an active hardening phase under RFC-0083.

## Delivered foundations

- contract-family inventory and route-family guard
- temporal vocabulary governance
- source-data product catalog governance
- market/reference target model
- security and lifecycle target model
- eventing and supportability target model
- production-readiness closure ledger

## Ongoing focus

- continue RFC-0083 slice execution
- reduce contract and documentation drift
- keep live supportability and control-plane evidence truthful
- keep app-local runtime support aligned with platform-owned shared infrastructure boundaries

## Intentional boundaries

- do not re-absorb cross-cutting ecosystem narrative that now belongs in `lotus-platform`
- do not drift into downstream analytics ownership
- do not let support or reporting convenience routes blur the contract-family model
